Jeff Bezos

Founder of Amazon and Blue Origin

Here's a timeline of Jeff Bezos's life:

  • 1964 - Born in Albuquerque, New Mexico
  • 1994 - Jeff Bezos founds Amazon.com
  • 2000 - Launches Amazon Web Services (AWS)
  • 2007 - Introduces the Kindle e-reader
  • 2013 - Acquires The Washington Post
  • 2018 - Becomes the world's wealthiest person
  • 2021 - Steps down as Amazon's CEO

"Jeff Bezos's impact on e-commerce and space exploration is significant, shaping the way we shop and expanding our horizons beyond Earth."

-- Wikipedia
